## Key Frameworks Quick Reference

### Valuation by Sector
| Sector | Primary Metric | Secondary |
|--------|---------------|-----------|
| Banks | P/Adj BV | ROA, NIM |
| Insurance | P/EV or Float DCF | Combined ratio |
| IT Services | PE or EV/EBITDA | TCV growth |
| Pharma/CDMO | PE + pipeline | USFDA track record |
| Hospitals | EV/EBITDA | EV/bed |
| Real Estate | EV/Pre-sales, NAV | Net debt/Pre-sales |
| Capital Goods | EV/EBITDA | Order book/Mcap |
| Commodities | EV/EBITDA (mid-cycle) | EV/tonne, Replacement cost |
| SaaS | EV/ARR | NRR, Rule of 40 |
| Cement | EV/tonne | EV/EBITDA |
| Shipping | EV/EBITDA | Day rates, BDI |

### Key Thresholds (Good Business)
- ROCE > 15-20% sustained | ROIIC > 15% (trend more important than level)
- CFO/PAT > 1x | Working capital days improving
- GNPA < 2% (banks) | PCR > 70% | NIM > 3.5%
- NRR > 115% (SaaS) | LTV/CAC > 3x
- ARPOB > Rs 40,000 (hospitals) | Occupancy > 65% (mature)
- PEG < 1.5x (GARP) | EV/EBITDA < 10x (base case for industrials)

### Red Flags (Universal)
- CFO consistently < PAT (earnings quality)
- Rising receivable days without revenue explanation
- Related party transactions growing faster than revenue
- Promoter pledge > 30% of holding
- Auditor change / qualified opinion / emphasis of matter
- ROIIC declining over 3-5 years
- Diversification into unrelated businesses (diworsification)
- Management missing guidance 2+ consecutive years
